# CAROUSEL SLIDE: Hook (Slide 1)

The hook slide is the most important — it determines whether the viewer swipes.
It must stop the scroll and create immediate curiosity or desire.

---

## PROMPT TEMPLATE

```
Social media carousel slide 1 of {{total_slides}}, hook slide, {{canvas_size}},
{{platform}} format. Part of a cohesive {{style_preset}} visual series.

BACKGROUND: Flat {{bg_color}} background, consistent with carousel system.
{{bg_accent}} subtle decorative element at {{accent_position}}, low opacity.

LEFT ACCENT BAR: Thin vertical rectangle, {{accent_bar_width}}px wide,
full height of canvas, positioned at left edge, flat {{accent_bar_color}} fill.
Present on every slide.

VISUAL HERO: {{hook_visual}} positioned {{hero_position}} of canvas,
{{hero_style}} treatment, {{hero_size}}% of canvas area. This should be the
most dramatic, eye-catching visual of the entire carousel sequence.
{{hero_effect}} for impact.

HEADLINE ZONE: {{headline_position}} — large flat {{headline_bg}} open area,
approximately 55% canvas width, 30% canvas height. Space for bold provocative
hook headline (question, bold claim, or surprising stat). No text rendered.

SUBTEXT ZONE: Immediately below headline zone — narrower flat strip, clean
{{subtext_bg}} area, space for 1-line supporting subtext or "swipe to learn more"
prompt. No text rendered.

SWIPE INDICATOR: Right edge center — small flat {{swipe_icon_color}} arrow or
chevron icon shape pointing right, with thin flat text bar below for "SWIPE"
label. Minimal, clean.

BOTTOM BRAND STRIP: Full-width bottom band, {{brand_strip_height}}px tall,
flat {{brand_strip_color}} fill. Left: flat {{logo_zone}} rectangle placeholder
for logo. Right: flat {{counter_bg}} circle, small, for slide number "1/{{total_slides}}".
No text rendered.

COLOR PALETTE: {{primary_color}}, {{accent_color}}, {{neutral_color}}.

STYLE: {{style_directive}}, maximum visual impact, scroll-stopping composition,
{{mood}} atmosphere, no text rendered, no numbers, no watermark.

CANVAS: {{canvas_size}}
```

---

## HOOK VISUAL STRATEGIES

| Hook Type | Visual Direction |
|---|---|
| Curiosity gap ("You won't believe...") | Split-reveal image, partial obscure |
| Bold stat ("95% of people...") | Large bold number graphic area, clean |
| Problem statement | Visual of pain point (cluttered desk, overwhelmed person) |
| Before/after promise | Split composition, left vs right contrast |
| Bold product reveal | Hero product, dramatic lighting, full impact |
| Question hook | Abstract visual that raises a question |

Choose the hook visual strategy based on the carousel topic and business goal.
