# CAROUSEL SLIDE: CTA Slide (Last Slide)

The CTA slide is the conversion moment. After delivering value through the
carousel, this slide asks for action. It should feel like a natural, satisfying
conclusion — not a jarring hard sell.

---

## PROMPT TEMPLATE

```
Social media carousel final slide {{slide_number}} of {{total_slides}}, call-to-
action slide, {{canvas_size}}, {{platform}} format. Part of cohesive
{{style_preset}} visual series.

BACKGROUND: {{cta_bg_style}} — {{cta_bg_description}}. This slide may use a
slightly elevated version of the carousel background for a "finale" feel:
{{cta_bg_color}}. Mood: conclusive, inviting, action-oriented.

LEFT ACCENT BAR: Same as all other slides — thin vertical rectangle,
{{accent_bar_width}}px, full height, left edge, flat {{accent_bar_color}}.

VISUAL ELEMENT: {{cta_visual}} positioned {{cta_visual_position}},
{{cta_visual_size}}% of canvas. {{cta_visual_style}} treatment.
Should reinforce the brand or outcome promised in the carousel.
Examples: product shot, happy person, brand graphic, achievement visual.

THANK YOU / CLOSING ZONE: Upper section — flat {{closing_zone_bg}} area,
small flat decorative element (heart icon, star, check icon) placeholder,
followed by flat text bars for closing message ("Thanks for reading!",
"Now you know", "Ready to start?"). No text rendered.

MAIN CTA HEADLINE ZONE: Center-dominant — large flat open area,
{{cta_headline_bg}} background, open space for CTA headline
("Ready to grow?", "Start today", "Book your free call").
Largest text zone on this slide. No text rendered.

PRIMARY CTA BUTTON: Prominent flat {{primary_button_color}} rounded rectangle,
centered, approximately 55% canvas width, clear button shape with strong
visual weight. Interior flat for CTA text overlay. {{button_style}} style.

SECONDARY CTA: Below primary button — thinner flat {{secondary_button_color}}
pill or outlined rectangle, 40% canvas width. Open for secondary action text
(follow, share, visit website). No text rendered.

SOCIAL PROOF ROW: Optional — horizontal flat strip between closing zone and
CTA headline, {{social_proof_count}} small flat avatar circle placeholders
in a row with flat text bar beside them ("Join 2,400+ members").
No text rendered.

LINK / HANDLE ZONE: Above bottom strip — flat open area for website URL or
@handle display. Small flat {{link_icon_color}} icon (link, globe, Instagram)
placeholder beside text bar. No text rendered.

BOTTOM BRAND STRIP: Full-width, {{brand_strip_height}}px, flat
{{brand_strip_color}} fill. Logo placeholder left. Right: flat
{{counter_bg}} circle — final slide number. No swipe indicator (last slide).

NO SWIPE INDICATOR on this slide — it is the final slide.

COLOR PALETTE: {{primary_color}}, {{accent_color}}, {{neutral_color}}.

STYLE: Warm, inviting, action-ready, {{mood}} energy, professional and
on-brand, no text rendered, no icons with embedded text, no watermark.

CANVAS: {{canvas_size}}
```

## CANVA EDITING ZONES

| Zone | Location | What to add |
|---|---|---|
| Closing message | Closing zone | "Thanks for reading!", "You made it!" |
| CTA headline | Headline zone | "Ready to [outcome]?" |
| Primary CTA | Primary button | "Book Now", "Enroll Today", "Shop Now" |
| Secondary CTA | Secondary pill | "Follow us", "Share this", "Save post" |
| Social proof | Social proof row | "Join X+ members" with avatar images |
| Website/handle | Link zone | Website URL or @socialhandle |
| Logo | Bottom strip left | Brand logo |
| Slide number | Bottom strip right | "5/5" or final count |
