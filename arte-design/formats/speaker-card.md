# FORMAT: Speaker Card

Use this template for: guest speaker announcements, podcast guest features,  
team member spotlights, expert introductions, panelist cards, author features.

---

## PROMPT TEMPLATE



```
Professional speaker announcement card, {{aspect_ratio}}, clean corporate design.

BACKGROUND: {{background_style}} — {{bg_color_primary}} base with {{bg_accent}}
decorative accent element (thin arc, quarter circle, diagonal stripe) in
{{bg_accent_color}}, positioned {{accent_position}}. Clean, minimal, professional.

PHOTO ZONE: {{photo_position}} of canvas — professional portrait photograph area,
person in {{attire_style}} attire, {{expression}} expression, {{photo_bg}}
background, photo integrated seamlessly with poster background via
{{photo_blend}} treatment. Photo occupies approximately {{photo_size}}% of canvas.

ROLE/EVENT BADGE: Small {{badge_color}} pill badge positioned near photo,
for role label such as "GUEST SPEAKER", "KEYNOTE", or "HOST".
May include generated text but must remain clean and easily replaceable.

NAME ZONE: {{name_position}} — large clean area for speaker full name in bold
typography. May include generated name but must remain highly readable and editable.

TITLE ZONE: Directly below name zone — structured strip for job title and
company/organization. May include generated text but must remain clean and replaceable.

COMPANY LOGO ZONE: Below title zone — {{logo_area_color}} rectangle,
clean space or placeholder mark for company logo overlay. Approximately
20% canvas width.

TOPIC / QUOTE ZONE: {{topic_position}} — structured panel with thin
{{accent_line_color}} vertical accent line, space for topic title or short
speaker quote. Optional decorative {{quote_mark_style}} quotation mark graphic
in low opacity. Text may be generated but must remain editable.

EVENT INFO STRIP: Bottom section — {{info_strip_color}} band, clean horizontal
strip divided into {{info_columns}} sections for event name, date, and platform.
Text may be generated but must remain readable and replaceable.

LOGO AREA (ORGANIZER): Top corner — may include a simple placeholder mark or
clean space for organizer logo replacement.

QR CODE AREA (optional): Small square zone for registration link —
generate as visual placeholder only.

TEXT HANDLING: If no text is provided, generate realistic speaker details
(name, role, company, topic, event info) suitable for a professional context.
All text must be clean, readable, and easily replaceable.

COLOR PALETTE: {{primary_color}}, {{accent_color}}, {{text_zone_color}}.

STYLE: Clean professional corporate design, editorial-quality portrait,
modern layout with generous whitespace, {{brand_feeling}} visual tone,
balanced composition, editable-friendly structure, no watermark.

ASPECT RATIO: {{aspect_ratio}}
```

## CANVA EDITING ZONES (UPDATED FOR MAGIC LAYERS)


| Zone               | Location         | What to do in Canva                |
| ------------------ | ---------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Speaker photo      | Photo zone       | Replace or refine portrait         |
| Role badge         | Near photo       | Adjust label text                  |
| Name               | Name zone        | Replace/refine full name           |
| Title              | Title zone       | Edit job title + company           |
| Company logo       | Logo area        | Replace placeholder with real logo |
| Topic              | Topic/quote zone | Adjust title or quote              |
| Event name         | Info strip       | Update event                       |
| Date               | Info strip       | Update date                        |
| Platform           | Info strip       | Update platform/location           |
| Organizer logo     | Top corner       | Replace with real logo             |
| QR code (optional) | Small area       | Replace with real QR               |
| Cleanup            | Entire layout    | Fix alignment, spacing, artifacts  |
