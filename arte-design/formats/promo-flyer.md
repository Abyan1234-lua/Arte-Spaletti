# FORMAT: Promo Flyer

Use this template for: sales promotions, discount announcements, flash sales,  
seasonal offers, bundle deals, limited-time campaigns, retail promotions.

---

## PROMPT TEMPLATE



```
Commercial promotional sale flyer, {{aspect_ratio}}, high visual energy.

BACKGROUND: {{background_style}} — {{bg_color_primary}} dominant base with
{{bg_color_secondary}} accent shapes or diagonal band. {{bg_decoration}}
decorative elements (stars, confetti, burst lines, ribbons) scattered across
background in {{decoration_color}}, small and non-distracting. Energetic,
eye-catching composition.

HERO PRODUCT / VISUAL: {{hero_description}} positioned {{hero_position}},
{{hero_size}}% of canvas, {{hero_treatment}} rendering style.
{{hero_effect}} surrounding element for energy and motion.

OFFER BADGE: Large {{badge_shape}} shape (starburst, circle, shield, ribbon)
positioned {{badge_position}}, flat {{badge_color}} fill with {{badge_outline}}
outline. Badge approximately 25% of canvas width. Interior kept clean for
discount or promo text. May include generated text but must remain editable.

HEADLINE ZONE: {{headline_position}} — wide flat open strip,
{{headline_bg_color}} color, clean space for promotional headline.
Height approximately 15% canvas. Text may be generated but must remain
clear and easy to refine.

PRODUCT SHOWCASE: {{product_count}} rectangular frames arranged in
{{grid_layout}} grid, each with {{frame_style}} styling. Can contain generated
product visuals or act as placeholder frames for replacement.

DEADLINE STRIP: Horizontal band near bottom, {{deadline_color}} fill,
clean space for validity or urgency text. Text may be generated but must
remain replaceable.

CTA BUTTON: Bottom center — {{cta_color}} rounded rectangle button shape,
prominent, approximately 40% canvas width. May include generated CTA text,
but must remain clear and editable.

LOGO AREA: Top-left or top-center — may include a simple placeholder mark or
clean space, intended for replacement with real brand logo.

QR CODE AREA (optional): Small square zone if needed — generate as visual
placeholder only.

TEXT HANDLING: If no text is provided, generate realistic promotional text
(headline, discount, CTA, deadline, product labels) appropriate to the offer.
All text must be clean, readable, and easily replaceable.

COLOR PALETTE: {{primary_color}}, {{secondary_color}}, {{accent_color}}.

STYLE: Bold commercial retail advertising, vibrant colors, high contrast,
energetic composition, {{print_or_digital}} quality, balanced layout,
editable-friendly structure, no watermark.

ASPECT RATIO: {{aspect_ratio}}
```

## CANVA EDITING ZONES (UPDATED FOR MAGIC LAYERS)


| Zone               | Location              | What to do in Canva                |
| ------------------ | --------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Brand logo         | Top-left / top-center | Replace placeholder with real logo |
| Headline           | Headline strip        | Refine or replace promo title      |
| Discount %         | Offer badge           | Adjust discount text               |
| Products           | Product grid          | Replace or refine product visuals  |
| Validity           | Deadline strip        | Update dates                       |
| CTA                | Bottom button         | Replace text + add link            |
| Social handle      | Bottom corner         | Add or refine handle               |
| QR code (optional) | Anywhere small        | Replace with real QR               |
| Cleanup            | Entire layout         | Remove artifacts, fix alignment    |
